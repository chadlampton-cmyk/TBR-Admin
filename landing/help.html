Terms of Use

Guidelines for using Doggfather Studio and Turnbuckle Report services.

Studio Access

Doggfather Studio is intended for authorized team members, invited guests, and approved production collaborators. Access credentials, invite links, and role permissions may not be shared outside approved use.

Acceptable Use

Users may not use the platform to disrupt recordings, interfere with other participants, upload unlawful material, attempt unauthorized access, or misuse chat, media, or guest tools.

Content Responsibility

Each user is responsible for the content they submit, record, upload, or distribute through the studio. You must have the rights and permissions necessary for any audio, video, image, or written content you use.

Account Control

Turnbuckle Report administrators may suspend access, revoke invites, remove content, or restrict features to protect studio operations, service integrity, or legal compliance.

Service Availability

We may update, limit, or interrupt features as part of maintenance, security response, or platform improvements. Continued use of the studio indicates acceptance of these operating terms.

Cookie Notice

How Doggfather Studio uses browser storage and related session technology.

Essential Session Use

Doggfather Studio uses essential browser storage and session data to keep users signed in, preserve secure access, and maintain live studio functionality such as chat, preferences, and role-aware controls.

Preference Storage

We store selected studio preferences such as device defaults, appearance settings, and workspace options so the platform can restore your working environment when you return.

Security and Verification

Browser data may also support security controls including login sessions, verification steps, access expiration, and fraud-prevention tooling needed to protect the studio and its users.

Managing Cookies

You may clear cookies or browser storage through your browser settings. Doing so may sign you out, reset saved preferences, or require you to complete authentication again.

Privacy Notice

What information is used to operate Doggfather Studio and support account security.

Information We Use

Doggfather Studio may use account details, profile information, session activity, login timestamps, invite status, and operational data required to authenticate users, manage access, and run studio features.

Operational Purpose

Information is used to provide secure sign-in, guest access, chat and presence features, diagnostics, moderation tools, and administrative oversight needed to operate the podcast production environment.

Limited Access

Access to administrative data is limited to authorized personnel with the permissions required to support production, security, and account management responsibilities.

Retention and Protection

Data may be retained for operational continuity, audit review, and security response. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect account and service information.

Support Contact

Questions about privacy or account access can be directed through the support contact configured for the studio or through Turnbuckle Report administration.
